Hack Horizon 2.0 - Terms and Conditions

  1. **Team Size:** Teams must consist of a minimum of 3 and a maximum of 5 participants.
  2. **Registration Fee:** A non-refundable fee of **₹800** per team is required for entry.
  3. **Payment Proof:** A clear screenshot or proof of payment must be attached during registration. Teams without valid proof will be disqualified.
  4. **Eligibility:** Participants must be currently enrolled university/college students.
  5. **Originality:** All submitted projects must be original work created entirely during the 24-hour hackathon period.
  6. **Code of Conduct:** All participants must adhere to the hackathon's Code of Conduct (available on the main event page). Harassment and discrimination will not be tolerated.
  7. **Intellectual Property:** Teams retain the intellectual property rights to their creations.
  8. **Judging:** Judges' decisions are final and binding.
